So... what's there to know about this website?

Angelina Savaiano
Angelina Savaiano is a passionate graphic designer studying at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. With a lifelong love for the arts, she’s explored painting, drawing, animation, video editing, and digital design—always seeking new ways to express creativity and tell visual stories.

Driven by curiosity and a desire to grow, Angelina continues to refine her skills and push creative boundaries. Her work blends bold design with thoughtful intention, aiming to inspire, connect, and leave a lasting impression.

Why create "Stargazing at Night"
Angelina Savaiano created this site to share her love for the stars and the quiet beauty of the night sky. Stargazing has always fascinated her, and though she wishes she could do it more often, she hopes this space encourages others to step outside and look up. With tips, reflections, and a passion for celestial wonder, Stargazing at Night invites visitors to slow down, reconnect with nature, and enjoy the simple magic of the stars.
