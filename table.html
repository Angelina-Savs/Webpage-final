More about the works!

# Artwork name Additional Information
1. Screen Depression (2023) Painted by Savaiano in 2023 over 10 focused hours, this gouache piece on thick multimedia paper explores digital disconnection through a reference photo of Angelina. Its icy blues and fiery oranges evoke the emotional paradox of online life—connection laced with isolation.
2. Bob Marley Moon (2025) Commissioned by the Bob Marley Hope Road Show, Savaiano transformed a real drum into a vibrant tribute—hand-painting a photo of Marley using acrylics over six focused hours. The finished piece now lives in the show’s connected retail space, blending music, art, and legacy in one unforgettable display.
3. Like Him? (2024) Created in Procreate, which took over 11 hours and 40 minutes, this digital painting was inspired by Tyler, the Creator’s “Like Him” song. It features two original characters close to Angelina’s heart—figures she hopes to one day bring to life in a published story.
4. Online Obsession (2023) Painted in 2023 over 15 focused hours, this gouache piece builds on Screen Depression’s theme of digital disconnection. Using reference photos of Angelina, it channels anxiety and emotional overload through vivid reds, eyeballs, and flowing wires that trap its users..
5. Oreo (2024) Drawn in 2024 using acrylic markers—Angelina’s first time experimenting with the medium—this piece came together in about an hour. Cut from multimedia paper to contour the artwork’s form, it was later gifted to the dog’s owner, turning a quick creative spark into a heartfelt keepsake.
6. Angelina Savaiano (2024) Digitally illustrated in 2024 for a GRC course at UNLV, this self-portrait was Angelina’s final project in Adobe Illustrator—designed to showcase her creative voice and technical skill. The prompt: depict yourself in any style, surrounded by five favorite things. Her picks? Plants, a VW car, chickens, Coca-Cola, and music—each woven into the composition as playful symbols of personality and passion.
